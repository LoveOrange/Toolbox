This is a user script that automatically toggles Zhihu's light/dark mode to match your system's color scheme preference. Say goodbye to manual theme switching and enjoy a seamless browsing experience that's easy on your eyes, day or night.

-   **Instant Start**: Runs at `document-start` to prevent Flash of Unstyled Content (FOUC).
